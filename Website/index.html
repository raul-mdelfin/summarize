Conference Summarization

About this App

The purpose of this application is to provide users a service that is able to summarize a conference, meeting, or lecture, and easily streamline the sumarization to all the participants in their native language.

We are using a multitude of API's in order to accomplish this task. Some of the features that are intergrated is the ability to include the emotional status of the speaker according to Microsoft Emotional cognitive service.
